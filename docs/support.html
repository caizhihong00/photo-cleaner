FAQ

Why can’t the app see my photos?

The app needs Photo Library permission. Open Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → photoCleaner, then allow access (or choose “Limited Access”).

I selected “Limited Access”. What happens?

With Limited Access, the app can only scan and manage the photos you explicitly allowed. You can expand access anytime in iOS Settings.

Can deleted items be restored?

Deleted items are removed from your Photo Library. Depending on your system settings, they may be available in “Recently Deleted” for a limited time. Always review before confirming deletion.

Does the app upload my photos?

No. photoCleaner processes your media on-device and does not upload your photos/videos to our servers.

Why is scanning slow?

Scanning speed depends on library size and whether some items are in iCloud. Keep the app open and connected to power/Wi‑Fi for best results.

Where can I report a bug?

Email support@example.com. If possible, attach a screenshot or screen recording.
